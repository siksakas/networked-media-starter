The Empty Bedroom


At first this was where all of us slept, then just my parents for a bit.

At night everyone who slept here complained about hearing footsteps or strange noises, and

my mom was convinced they came from antique items my dad brought home.



I never really believed in it, but whenever I was alone in this room I'd always feel uneasy and

try to leave right away. It wasn't that I thought the room felt haunted, it just felt weird.
