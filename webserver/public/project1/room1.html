The Buddha Room


The Buddha room, or the prayer room, is the only room of which I have no photos.

I only went in maybe five or six times my entire time living here. There were maybe

fifty statues all lined up, some stacked higher than others.


What I remember is a hidden cabinet behind one of the statues, which opened when

you push on the wall. I once saw my Dad open it, but I never looked inside myself.

The room always felt weird to me, it was always cold even in the Thai summer heat.


I found a similar looking room here, but this one is less populated by statues.
