Living Room


We spent all of our freetime in this room. Me and my sister would play games here all day, play with

our toys, ride around the room in our little bikes.



I loved reading, and the bookshelf in the background of the photo was later swapped with a bigger one.



Before I was maybe 3-4 this was the only room other than the bedroom I spent a lot of time in, in the house. Behind those

gates to leave it was always dark so I didn't want to leave anyways, but my sister always tried to climb over them.

I remember laughing at her attempts to climb over, especially because she never gave up.
