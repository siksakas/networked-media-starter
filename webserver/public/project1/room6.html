Stairway


The stairway I'm writing about is in the background, and the weird thing was

that my parents locked entry to it with a fingerprint scanner. Only a few people

could unlock it, not me or my two sisters, they said it would stop us from getting

hurt going down the stairs.



It is a bit hard to see in the photo since it is blurry, but between the two paintings you can see

the scanner on the other side. I always thought that was a weird explanation.



Downstairs was a second living room along with another entrance to the apartment.

This next part seems crazy, and I'll understand if you do not believe me, but behind where the TV was

mounted was a hidden room. I didn't even think about it until I began writing this because it wasn't crazy

for me at the time, but when you pressed on the wall behind the TV it would open a bit and you could pull

the door open. Inside was nothing special: old toys, old books, stuff we'd never use on a daily basis.

My dad described it as somewhere we could hide if someone broke in, but that seems unlikely considering we

lived on the 22nd floor.
