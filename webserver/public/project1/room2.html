Bedroom


At first this was just me and my sisters room, until my parents moved to sleep in there too.

Even though our parents slept in the same room as us, my sister and I always went to sleep without

them. They'd return home from work after we were asleep and left before we woke up.



Other than a cabinet, the only pieces of furniture were two large mattresses which we

slept on. I remember my sister would always set the temperature to something too low and I would

wake up freezing.



The special thing about our bedroom was the glass wall. I could always see the silhouettes of people

in the livingroom while I was lying in bed.


My parents moved to sleep in this room when they started hearing weird noises in the other room.

The weird thing is when they moved bedrooms the noises seemed to move with them too, and I begun to

hear the same things they did at night.
